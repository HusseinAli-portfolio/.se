About Me

Behind The Camera

I'm a final-year student in film and TV production, driven by a passion for visual storytelling. My journey began with curiosity and a camera, exploring the power of moving images to capture human emotion and complex narratives. Today, I craft short films and projects that challenge perspectives and resonate with audiences on a deeper level.

I focus on cinematography, directing, and post-production, developing a distinctive visual language in every frame. Through my studies and practical work, I continue to refine my craft and explore new ways to bring stories to life.
